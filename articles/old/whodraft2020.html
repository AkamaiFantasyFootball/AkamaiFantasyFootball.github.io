Who Did I Draft? - 2020

Reyn Yamamoto - 09/11/20

It wasn’t always like this. It used to be a game that I would play with my friends in school. I didn’t follow the offseason or coaching changes if it didn’t involve my team. There were times during the draft; I would look at my printed Top 200 list and not realize a particular player switched teams or gotten injured in the preseason. How times have changed. For the first time, I’ve crossed into the double digits. Managing 12 leagues seem daunting but motivated by ambition; I’m enjoying the experience so far.

With more teams than toes, I wanted to determine which players I drafted the most. I joined various leagues with many settings: half/full PPR & standard scoring, 6 points per passing TD, 2 QB/Superflex, auction drafts, tight end premium scoring, guillotine league, and a dynasty startup. I also drafted from every end of the draft board. It turns out that even I have a specific group of guys that just happened to fall to me in a third of my teams. If you see your player here, you can pat yourself on the back or slap the back of your head for drafting the same players.

I recommend tier-based drafting when there's depth at a position; it allows you to make an easy decision while maximizing value.
